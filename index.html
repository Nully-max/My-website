04 — FAQ

Common
questions.

Do you deliver the source code with the project?
Yes, always. Every web project and game project ships with its full, commented source code. You own what you paid for — no vendor lock-in, no hidden dependencies on me.
What kind of websites can you build?
Primarily business websites (restaurants, cafes, local services), personal portfolios, and landing pages. I handle the full stack — design, frontend, backend, and deployment. If you have a specific idea, reach out and we'll figure out if it's a fit.
Do you offer maintenance after launch?
Yes. I offer reliable post-launch maintenance support. Whether it's content updates, bug fixes, or small feature additions, I stay available after the project ships.
Can you make a game for my project or brand?
Potentially yes — depends on the scope. I work in Godot and can build 2D or 3D games. Casual games, jam-style games, browser games, and interactive experiences are all within reach. Let's talk about what you have in mind.
How do we communicate during the project?
Primarily by email at abdouboners@gmail.com. I'm also open to using Discord or whatever platform works best for you. I aim to respond within 24 hours.
Are you available for international clients?
Absolutely. I'm based in Bizerte, Tunisia, and I work with clients remotely. I'm very fluent in English, so communication is no barrier. Time zone differences are manageable with async communication.
What's your background?
I'm a CS student with hands-on experience in web development and game development. I placed 48th out of 15,000+ entries in the Brackeys Game Jam, one of the largest indie game jams in the world. I'm passionate about building real, working products — not just side projects that never ship.
